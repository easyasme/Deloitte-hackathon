7. MODEL LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

7.1 NEGATIVE PREDICTIONS
Some low-exposure ZIPs produce negative premium predictions.
This occurs because:
  - Low exposure areas have high variance in premium data
  - Ridge/Panel models can extrapolate below zero
  - These should be clipped to $0 in production

7.2 WILDFIRE RISK RELATIONSHIP
Surprisingly, "Avg Fire Risk Score" has low importance (0.004).
This suggests:
  - Premium pricing may not fully reflect fire risk
  - Or fire risk is already priced into exposure volume
  - Further investigation needed for actuarial implications

7.3 TIME SERIES CONSTRAINTS
  - Only 3 years of training data limits trend detection
  - COVID-19 impact (2020) may distort 2021 predictions
  - Structural breaks not captured by standard models
